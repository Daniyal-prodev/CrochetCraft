Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my purchased patterns?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with download links. You can also access your patterns from your account dashboard at any time.

Can I sell items made from your patterns?

Yes! You can sell finished items made from our patterns. However, you cannot redistribute, share, or sell the patterns themselves.

What if I need help with a pattern?

We offer full support for all our patterns! Contact us via email or join our community forum where fellow crafters and our team can help you.

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the digital nature of our products, we don't offer refunds. However, if you're having issues with a pattern, please contact us and we'll work to resolve any problems.

How often do you release new patterns?

We release new patterns weekly! Subscribe to our newsletter to be the first to know about new releases and special offers.
